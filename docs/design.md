# VocabNote - UI Design System

## Theme
Modern, clean, and distraction-free Dark Mode.

## Colors
*   **Background:** True Black (`#000000`) or very dark grey (`#111111`) to reduce eye strain.
*   **Accent Color:** Blue (`#1F6AA5` or similar). Used for primary buttons (Save, Export), active tabs, and important highlights.
*   **Text:** White or light grey for high readability.

## Layout Structure
*   **Left Sidebar:** Navigation (My Notebook, Add Word, Settings).
*   **Top Bar:** Search box, A-Z filter, and the always-visible Export button.
*   **Main Content Area:** Displays the list of words or the details of a specific word.
